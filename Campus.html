Our Sacred Learning Space

Experience the tranquility of our traditional Gurukul campus nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas.

Campus Overview

A harmonious blend of traditional architecture and natural surroundings

Our Gurukulam campus is designed following ancient Vastu principles, creating an environment conducive to learning and spiritual growth. Spread across 10 acres of lush greenery, the campus offers a serene atmosphere where students can immerse themselves in Vedic studies away from the distractions of modern life.

The campus architecture reflects traditional Indian design elements, with spacious courtyards, meditation halls, and learning spaces that blend harmoniously with nature. Every aspect of the campus is thoughtfully designed to support the holistic development of our students.

Our Facilities

Modern amenities within a traditional learning environment

Residential Quarters

Simple, comfortable living spaces designed for focus and reflection. Each residence houses 4-6 students with shared amenities and study areas.

Learning Halls

Traditional open halls (Shalas) where students gather to learn from teachers, with natural lighting and ventilation creating an ideal learning atmosphere.

Meditation Spaces

Dedicated areas for meditation and spiritual practices, including a central temple and various smaller shrines throughout the campus.

Organic Gardens

Extensive herb and vegetable gardens where students learn sustainable farming practices while growing food for the campus kitchen.

Sattvic Kitchen

Our kitchen serves pure vegetarian, sattvic meals prepared with ingredients from our organic gardens, following traditional Ayurvedic principles.

Ancient Library

A collection of rare manuscripts, Vedic texts, and contemporary resources on Sanskrit, philosophy, and ancient wisdom traditions.

Finding Us

Our campus is located in the serene foothills of Rishikesh

Address

Gurukulam Campus, Near Laxman Jhula,

Getting Here

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport (Dehradun), approximately 35 km from our campus.
  • By Train: Rishikesh Railway Station is 12 km from our campus. We can arrange pickup on request.
  • By Road: Regular buses connect Rishikesh to major cities like Delhi (240 km) and Haridwar (25 km).

Shuttle Service

We provide a free shuttle service from Rishikesh town to our campus at scheduled times. Please contact us in advance to arrange pickup.
